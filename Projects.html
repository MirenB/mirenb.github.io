Projects

I had the opportunity to work on different projects in the university, as well as I had the chances to do volunteered internships. University projects

Zundak (2019)

Zundak was a cyberjournalism project leaded by three friends and I. In Zundak I wrote several news and I was in charge of the social media platforms. At the same time, I did my social media administration course, and I had the chance to apply what I learned in there.

Staycation in Utrecht (2020)

Staycation in Utrecht was a project for a course called Persuasive Content Marketing, with the goal of gathering audience for a hotel chain. The platforms that I used were Twitter and a podcast with the same name that can be heard in Spotify.

Internships

During four months (February-June, 2021) I did my internships on the audio-visual agency Media Attack, located in Bilbao. While working there, I was a Comunity Manager for several local city hall websites and social media. There I learned how to use Word Press and Elementor and I put in practice the planning of content calendars for Twitter, Facebook and Instagram.
